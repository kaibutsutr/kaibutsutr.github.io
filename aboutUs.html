About Us

    Destannet is a Turkish Internet Service Provider (ISP) company that provides services on both its own infrastructure and National infrastructure (Turk Telekom)

    First steps were taken in 2018 in order to provide service on its own infrastructure. DestanNet started its services in 31.5.2019 after taking an ISP license from Information Technologies and Communication Authority (BTK)

    DestanNet achieved great success after creating its own metro network at different points of Turkey in cooperation with Turkcell Superonline in 2019.

    DestanNet provides services and solutions to most of its customers over its own infrastructure. Furthermore, it provides internet and data services to everywhere in Turkey in cooperation with Turkcell Superonline and Turktelekom.
